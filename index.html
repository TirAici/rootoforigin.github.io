Why the Root of Origin?

The Root of Origin is one of the many names of the world tree, Al-Saheyreh. Her roots reach beyond the world and anchor and entwine reality and the Dream. It is from this link to The First Dream that all realities, all things are simulataneously existant and recorded.

But what does that mean?

The root of origin is the core of a metaphysical network that stretches throughout the world through which all knowledge, memories and possibilities are recorded. Sound familiar?
